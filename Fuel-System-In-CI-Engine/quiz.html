Engine Systems Quiz

Test your knowledge with these fill-in-the-blank questions

Fill in the Blanks Quiz

1. In a diesel engine, only ____ is drawn in during the suction stroke.

air

2. During the compression stroke, the air is ____.

compressed

3. Fuel is injected in the form of a ____ at the end of the compression stroke.

fine spray

4. The fuel injection system must inject fuel at the ____ time in the cycle.

right

5. Proper ____ of fuel ensures efficient combustion.

atomization

6. The quantity of fuel injected depends on the engine ____.

load

7. The main function of the ____ is to store fuel for the engine.

fuel tank

8. The ____ removes dirt and impurities from the fuel before it reaches the engine.

fuel filter

9. A ____ is used to separate water from diesel fuel.

water separator

10. The fuel pump is responsible for ____ fuel from the tank to the injectors.

pumping

11. High-pressure pumps in diesel engines include Inline FIP, Rotary Pump, and ____.

High-Pressure Pump (CRDI)

12. ____ are commonly used in Common Rail Direct Injection (CRDI) systems.

EDC pumps

13. The ____ injects fuel into the combustion chamber at high pressure.

fuel injector

14. CRDI stands for ____.

Common Rail Direct Injection

15. The CRDI system improves ____ efficiency and reduces emissions.

fuel

16. A turbocharger with a ____ mechanism helps regulate boost pressure.

wastegate

17. A ____ turbocharger has two separate exhaust gas inlets for better performance.

twin-scroll

18. A ____ adjusts airflow dynamically for better engine efficiency.

variable geometry turbocharger (VGT)

19. An ____ turbocharger uses electric power to reduce lag and provide instant boost.

electric
