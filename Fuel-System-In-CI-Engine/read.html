Fuel Supply System in CI Engine

Quick Glance

The fuel supply system in CI engines ensures precise fuel delivery, proper atomization, and accurate timing for optimal combustion performance.

Core Concepts

Overview

Only air enters during suction stroke, fuel injected at end of compression stroke

Components

Consists of fuel tank, pumps, filters, injectors, and supply lines

Process

Ensures precise timing, atomization, and quantity control

Main Components

Primary Components

  • Fuel pump
  • Fuel line
  • Fuel filters

Advanced Components

  • Water separator
  • Fuel injection pump
  • High pressure pump (CRDI)
  • EDC pump
